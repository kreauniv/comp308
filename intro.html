Welcome note¶

This course introduces students to common principles underlying various programming langauges through the process of constructing an interpreter for a language incrementally. It uses the PLAI second edition as the base material and is adapted to suit Krea University students and timeline. It is taught using Racket #lang plai-typed as well.

Students wished to use images as an alternative to working with numbers. Hence the course plan changed a bit to reflect that. The current course notes can be accessed at https://kreauniv.github.io/comp308/ .
